Bulk upload

The Office for National Statistics (ONS)

The Office for National Statistics (ONS) releases geographic data on a quarterly basis and this must be updated in the Get Information about Schools (GIAS) service.

The bulk Office for National Statistics (ONS) upload is a quick way to make the many changes to the geographic data, which includes historic and current postcodes in England and Wales for current administrative, electoral and census areas.

Download the template spreadsheet, make the required changes offline and then upload the data into GIAS.

How to prepare the spreadsheet

For a step by step guide on how to prepare the spreadsheet for bulk update please refer to the Office for National Statistics (ONS) bulk update guidance.

Download template and produce file

Download the spreadsheet ONS bulk update template.

Update the spreadsheet template with the relevant information and save the file, following the naming conventions in the guidance document.

Upload spreadsheet file

Upload the saved file to Get Information about Schools (GIAS) by selecting the choose file button below so that you can find where you saved it.

Effective date (optional)

The effective date is the date that the uploaded ONS data takes effect and can be set to either the current date or a date in the future.

For example, 27 3 2007
