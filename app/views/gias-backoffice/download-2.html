Data downloads and extracts

The downloads are publicly available files of GIAS data and are updated daily. The downloads are available as zipped CSV files and users have the option to download all or individually selected files. The data can be historically downloaded from September 11, 2020 to the current day.

More information
The order of the files and order/inclusion of the data items in the files may change. Where this happens we will give users advance notice through the GIAS news page. We would advise using data item codes where available.

Contents

  • Establishment, group and governance data
  • Scheduled extracts
